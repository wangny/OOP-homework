1.Program Description
	i. There are three Characters, when user lunches the game, two Characters will be random chosen and shown on the screen, every character has their own special skill.
	ii. Character may chose any skill when it's its turn too attack, every skill will have an accuracy rate. If do effect, the damage will multiple with an coefficient 1.5 or 0.5 depends on the Type of skill and its opponent.
	iii. There's animation  if it's the character's turn to attack.
